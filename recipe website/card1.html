Ingredients

  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced bell peppers (red, green, or yellow)
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 2-3 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For Frying (Optional):
    Vegetable oil for frying


    For the Dipping Sauce:
    1/4 cup soy sauce

Recipe

  1. Prepare the Filling:

    In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, grated carrots, thinly sliced bell peppers, bean sprouts, and sliced spring onions.

  2. Season the Filling:

    Add the minced garlic and grated fresh ginger to the bowl with the vegetables. Drizzle in the soy sauce, sesame oil, and vegetable oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Toss and Mix:

    Toss the ingredients in the bowl until the vegetables are evenly coated with the seasoning.

  4. Prepare the Spring Roll Wrappers:

    Take a spring roll wrapper and place it on a clean, flat surface. If you're using rice paper, dip it briefly in warm water to soften (follow the package instructions).

  5. Add the Filling:

    Place a portion of the vegetable filling near the bottom third of the wrapper, leaving some space on the sides.

  6. Roll the Spring Roll:

    Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling. Start rolling from the bottom, tucking the filling in as you go, until you have a neat, compact roll.

  7. Seal the Spring Roll:

    To seal the roll, wet the top edge of the wrapper with a little water and press it down to seal the spring roll.

  8. Repeat the Process:

    Repeat the process with the remaining wrappers and filling.

  9. Optional: Frying (or Serve Fresh):

    You can either serve the spring rolls fresh as they are or choose to fry them for a crispier texture. If frying:

    1. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Carefully lower the spring rolls into the hot oil and fry until they are golden brown and crispy.
    3. Remove them from the oil and place them on paper towels to drain excess oil.

    In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, and chili garlic sauce. Adjust the amount of chili sauce to your desired level of spiciness.

  11. Serve:

    Serve the vegetable spring rolls with the dipping sauce on the side.
